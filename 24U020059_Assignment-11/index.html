About the Institute

Building Visionary Technologists

Established under the IIIT (PPP) Act 2017, IIIT Bhopal is a premier technical institute dedicated to nurturing innovation and excellence. Situated in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, we shape future-ready engineers and researchers.

Our mission is to provide world-class education, foster groundbreaking research, and build bridges between academia and industry. We are committed to creating a vibrant intellectual community where ideas thrive.
