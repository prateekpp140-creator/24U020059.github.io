About the Conference

Shaping the Future of Data Science

ICADS 2025, organized by the Indian Institute of Information Technology Bhopal, is a premier platform for advancing knowledge in data science, artificial intelligence, and computational intelligence.

The conference fosters collaboration between academia and industry, presenting research that addresses real-world challenges through data-driven innovation. With a strong peer-review process and world-class keynote speakers, ICADS 2025 promises to be a landmark event.

Papers accepted and presented at ICADS 2025 will be published in IEEE Xplore and indexed in Scopus.

🏛️ About IIIT Bhopal

Indian Institute of Information Technology Bhopal is a premier institute of national importance established under the IIIT (PPP) Act 2017. Located in the heart of India, IIITDM Jabalpur is renowned for its research excellence in computer science and data engineering.

For Authors

Paper Submission

Follow these steps to submit your research paper for consideration at ICADS 2025.

1

Prepare Your Manuscript

Use the IEEE double-column template. Maximum 8 pages including references. Ensure proper anonymization for blind review.

2

Create EasyChair Account

Register at EasyChair.org and log in. Navigate to the ICADS 2025 conference submission portal.

3

Submit Abstract First

Submit a 250-word abstract with keywords by July 15, 2025. Full paper submission opens after abstract approval.

4

Upload Full Paper

Upload your PDF by July 31, 2025. Ensure file size is under 10MB and all fonts are embedded.

5

Track Your Submission

Monitor your submission status on EasyChair. Reviewers' comments will be available post-deadline.

6

Revise & Finalize

Incorporate reviewer feedback and submit the camera-ready version by October 10, 2025.

For queries: submissions@icads2025.iiitbhopal.ac.in
