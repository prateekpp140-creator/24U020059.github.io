It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (ICADS 2025), hosted at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Bhopal. This conference serves as a premier platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share cutting-edge advances in AI, machine learning, and data-driven technologies.
